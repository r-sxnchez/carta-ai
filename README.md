# Carta

> **AI-powered civic infrastructure for constitutional verification in Latin America.**

Carta helps citizens verify political claims using constitutional reasoning, transparent evidence, and explainable AI.

Built for a region where misinformation spreads through WhatsApp forwards, screenshots, viral clips, and political chains.

---

## 🌎 What is Carta?

Carta is a constitutional verification platform designed to combat political misinformation in Latin America.

Instead of asking users to blindly trust AI, Carta shows:

* Constitutional evidence
* Institutional context
* Traceable reasoning
* Relevant legal articles
* Manipulation signals

The goal is not to tell people what to think.

The goal is to help citizens understand:

* what is constitutionally plausible
* what institutional limits exist
* what context viral claims omit

---

## ⚖️ Why Carta?

In Latin America, misinformation spreads conversationally.

Most political manipulation happens through:

* WhatsApp forwards
* screenshots
* TikToks
* tweets
* edited clips
* out-of-context political statements

Traditional fact-checking is often:

* too slow
* too technical
* too centralized
* difficult to access

Carta brings constitutional verification directly into conversational environments.

---

## 📲 Core Experience

### WhatsApp-first verification

Users can:

1. Send a political claim through WhatsApp
2. Receive contextual constitutional analysis
3. Open a detailed evidence explorer on the web
